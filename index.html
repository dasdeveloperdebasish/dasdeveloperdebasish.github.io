My approach

How I work

Things I believe in — learned from 5 years of shipping real products.

01
Production first, not just demos

Every decision considers real users on real devices — mid-range Android, slow 4G, app restarts. These are my test cases, not just the latest iPhone on WiFi.

02
Measure before optimising

I never guess what is slow. React DevTools Profiler, Sentry, and real device testing tell me what is actually the problem before I touch anything.

03
Architecture that scales

Features added six months later should not break what exists. I build state management, navigation, and component architecture that stays clean as the project grows.

04
AI tools, human judgment

I use Cursor AI and GitHub Copilot daily. But I read, debug, and take responsibility for every line that goes to production. AI is a tool, not a replacement for thinking.
